one more thing, before you go

Dad — if we could put just one thing in this letter, it's thank you. Thank you for the years you made look easy, even on the days they weren't. For showing up, every single time, even when showing up was the hard part.

For teaching us things you never actually said out loud — the kind you only learn from watching someone do them, again and again, without ever asking for credit.

I hope this small page does what it really can't: hold even a fraction of that. Happy Father's Day.

— your family
